Information & Tutorials for the Player

Lobby

Before you know what's happening, you spawn into what you assume to be some type of lobby. You feel dizzy and light headed, but you are strong willed and attempt to figure out the roots of this place. Traversing through this new world you find a coin, then another, then another... Further along your path you find what appear to be enterances to some type of dwelling or cave. Being the curious adventurer that you are, you decide to check out what lies ahead. What are these enterances and where do they lead?
